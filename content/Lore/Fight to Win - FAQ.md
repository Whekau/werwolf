
### How does a werewolf pack feasibly bring down a midsize company?
*Businesses need to be able to function to survive. Salaries need to be paid, and money has to come in. If the Garou successfully sabotage the basic functioning of a business, it eventually has to close.*

**Examples:**
* ram a supply truck into another, causing material losses.
* identifying political weaknesses that could be exploited, such as company support for an unpopular politician.
* analyze the company so that any acts of sabotage cause maximum damage.


### What are the limits of violence?
*The Garou are warriors of immense power, but not every challenge can be met with claw or fang. Yet surprisingly often, a little judiciously applied violence goes a long way. Identifying supernatural threats is key: destroying fomori, banishing Banes, and killing Black Spiral Dancers typically results in a much cleaner environment, and human allies can often take it from there.*

**Examples:**
* fell a tree to block a road so human reinforcements won’t be able to arrive in time to help a paramilitary squad of fomori. This way, battlefield control helps keep loss of life to a minimum.
* Threaten a group of Black Spiral Dancers and their human sycophants, and soon only the werewolves remain. Faced with the Garou, humans have a tendency to run.


### How do you recognize a supernatural threat?
*Garou have killed innocent humans, believing them to be fomori, more than once. This is why checking what things look like in the Umbra, trying to provoke supernatural manifestations, and listening to the advice of trusted spirits are all worthwhile methods. Better to avoid repeating the mistakes of the past.*

### How to find human allies?
*A key problem for many Garou is that potential human allies, i.e., humans who share goals similar
to the veneration for Gaia, often reject violence. For this reason, the most successful tactic may be anonymity. The Garou can keep a popular, ecologically minded politician alive despite the best efforts of an Albion Petroleum hit squad to kill him without having to reveal themselves. Just figure out which humans fight the good fight and make sure they can keep fighting. That said, a solid human identity is very helpful for interacting with potential allies.*
